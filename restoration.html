RESTORATION

Have you ever ripped a hole in your pants? Or tore your favorite sock? If so, I'm.. really sorry, but I do have good news! When an article of clothing is damaged, due to its makeup, it can also be repaired. This might seem like somewhat of a daunting task, but it's actually not only very simple to try out, but a great skill to have.

Textile restoration is the practice and process of restoring textiles to their original condition (or to another one that otherwise "fixes" damage). If you're curious what a 'textile' is, it's any item made from natural or synthetic fibers! This includes not only clothes like a pair of mittens or a favorite coat, but also curtains, bedspreads, insulation for our homes, baskets, hot air balloons, and so much more!

Fabrics + Textiles are highly versatile and their conservation is incredibly important to preserving our cultures and histories. On a smaller scale, however, being able to repair your own clothes is super neat and can be an engaging, helpful hobby! Never fear for your lucky sock, you can give it a second life!
