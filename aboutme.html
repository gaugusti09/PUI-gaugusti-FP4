ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Hi! My name is August :*o)!! I'm a current senior here @ CMU studying Information Systems, HCI, and Interactive Media Design. In addition to being a student, one of my most beloved hobbies is textile arts! Textile arts encompass a lot of different things, some you may be very familiar with like knitting or crochet, but other practices as well such as quilting, needlefelting, or mending. I made this website to showcase my love for textile arts and why I believe they're so special. I hope you enjoy!
