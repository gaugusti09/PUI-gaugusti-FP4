DO IT YOURSELF: DARNING & MORE

If you're interested in learning to mend your own clothes, here are some helpful links to get started! Start with learning to sew and basic stitches, then move on to patching and/or mending, and after that, the world is your oyster. Making your own clothes is never too far off either and I encourage giving that a try as well! Crafting communities are very strong, both online and off -- a local tailor (Old Flame Mending) frequently hosts workshops in Pittsburgh and they are super beginner friendly! Sometimes, local libraries offer classes as well and it's a great way to engage in your community while learning a new skill.

Happy Crafting!!
